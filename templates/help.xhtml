1. Clicking on this area, slider opens and let's you choose desired level.
  2. Clicking on this area, slider opens and let's you choose desired level range.
  3. Clicking on this area (3a), map opens (3b) and let's you choose desired realm(s) and its part(s). If you want to search specific realm, click on realm logo below the map. If you wish to search in specific areas of realms (Initiation, Inner and War zones), simply click on map to select desired zone(s). You can select multiple zones.
  4. If you wish to search by MOB, press button "By Mob". It is useful when looking for a MOB to level on. If you click button "By Area", you get a list of the best area spots for a grind.
  5. Here the search result is displayed (5a). If you wish to see detailed information for a particular search result from a list, click "+" button (5b), left from a search result name.
  6. Your feedback keeps tools alive! To submit it, click on "Feedback" (6), press button "Start" in opened dialogue, and follow wizard steps.

    If you need help with tools' user interface, click "Help". A brief guide opens in a dialogue.
  7. More about tools' license, you can read by clicking "Read License Information".
