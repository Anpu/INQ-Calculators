Welcome to the Inquisition Clan Database!

Here you can find some useful tools to help you in Regnum Online.
List of the tools:
  • Levels: calculate the number of mob kills till you level up.
  • Mobs: search for the mobs by name.
  • Areas: find the best area for a grind.
  • Pets: find the pet for your hunter.
  • NPCs: search for the quest NPCs.
  • Shops: find out which merchants sell stuff for your class.
  • Armor: calculate armor points per damage type of your armor set.
  • Trainer: build the setup for your character.
  • Credits: list who provided you all this.
